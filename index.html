ABOUT ME

Hello, nice to meet you! I am...

Tiffany Yu, currently a sophomore majoring in Computer Science and Business Administration at the University of Southern California.

I was born in Torrance, California but I grew up in Kaohsiung, Taiwan. I was brought up speaking Mandarin to my parents, English to my older sister and friends, and Taiwanese to my grandmother.

Because I grew up surrounded by different languages and dialects, I’ve always been drawn to unique forms of communication. It also led me to pursue such contrasting areas of study.

In my free time, I like to browse Uber Eats, doodle on my iPad, and dance in front of the bathroom mirror. I am also interested in learning more about product management, product design, and marketing. If you have any questions or comments, feel free to contact me! I’d love to have a chat. :)
